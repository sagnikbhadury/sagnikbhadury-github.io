<span style="text-align: justify">I am a 4<sup>th</sup> year PhD student in **_Division of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics_** at <span style ="color:green;text-decoration:none">[Penn State College of Medicine](https://med.psu.edu/)</span>. <span style="text-align: justify"> Before joining Penn State, I completed **_B.Sc.
